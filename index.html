Experience Clarity

Experience clarity with Rivelin Aligners - they're nearly invisible yet highly impactful. Customized to fit your smile, these aligners gently and effectively guide your teeth into perfect alignment, revealing a dazzling smile. With Rivelin Aligners and your orthodontist's expertise, your signature smile will shine. It's not just about appearance; it's an investment in your oral health and well-being.

ADVANTAGES OF RIVELIN ALIGNERS

Discreet

Rivelin aligners are virtually invisible, crafted from transparent polymer materials, ensuring they go unnoticed by others while you wear them.

Comfortable

Our advanced Rivelin software calculates tooth movement precisely, minimizing any discomfort to oral soft tissues often caused by traditional fixed brackets and wires.

Removable

Enjoy the freedom of removing your Rivelin aligner for thorough oral cleaning, reducing the risk of cavities and mineral loss.

Convenient

Bid farewell to bands, wires, and brackets – Rivelin aligners simplify the process, saving chair-side time and extending the intervals between visits.

Visualized Results

Through our cutting-edge 3D interface, the Rivelin Scheme Viewer visually guides your treatment journey with animated stages.

ACHIEVE YOUR BEAUTIFUL SMILE IN 6 SIMPLE STEPS!

Step 1: Comprehensive Clinic Examination

Visit one of Rivelin's partner Doctor for a thorough oral examination, including facial and intraoral photography, X-rays, and either intraoral scanning or traditional impression making.

1
2

Step 2: Tailored Treatment Plan

Our expert orthodontist team at Rivelin Center crafts a personalized 3D digital treatment plan based on your tooth models, offering customized orthodontic solutions.

Step 3: Approval of 3D Plan

Experience your treatment journey come to life as the doctor interprets your plan through 3D animation, allowing you to visualize each movement of your teeth and the final result.

3
4

Step 4: Custom Aligner Creation

Once your treatment plan is approved, Rivelin utilizes cutting-edge 3D printing technology to fabricate your customized aligners. Wear them as instructed by your doctor.

Step 5: Regular Check-ins

Stay on track with your treatment progress by scheduling regular check-ups as advised by your doctor. These visits are crucial for achieving the perfect treatment outcome.

5
6

Step 6: Retainers

Congratulations on completing your treatment! As per your doctor's advice, we recommend wearing Rivelin retainers to maintain your beautiful smile for years to come.
